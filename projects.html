Nosphere (Canceled)

Nosphere was a mobile phone game, you controlled different characters around the edge of a circle to attack oncoming enemies. It had a shop for unlockable characters as well as different environments you could play in.

This project was canceled in early 2016 after I recieved a HTC Vive and wanted to focus on a new project surrounding VR.
