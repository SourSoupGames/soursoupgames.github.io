2017-2018

Spells 'n' Stuff Remake (Name set to change)

Due to issues with the netcode of the first version of the game, I decided it would be easier to properly plan out and remake the entire game than try to fix what was already there. I have been trying to work on this inbetween attending college, not only on the VR version but also on an FPS version (Keyboard and Mouse).

2016

Spells 'n' Stuff (Waiting on the remake)

Spells 'n' Stuff was an online VR-based wizard arena shooter, the aim of the game is to fire spells at each others heads to turn them into ghosts. This also had a singleplayer mode in which the player fought waves of goblins who are trying to steal the player's gold.

2015

Nosphere (Abandoned)

Nosphere was a mobile phone game, you controlled different characters around the edge of a circle to attack oncoming enemies. It had a shop for unlockable characters as well as different environments you could play in.
This project was abandoned in early 2016 after I recieved a HTC Vive from Valve and wanted to focus on a new project surrounding VR.
