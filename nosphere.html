NOSPHERE is an arcade Shoot 'em Up, having you terminate enemies about a circle!

Ward off enemies that come in from all directions - whether you play as Grandma, protecting her pie from robbers, or Max the Dog, keeping his bone away from a skeleton army!

NOSPHERE has received a PEGI 7 preliminary age rating for Google Play.

I have decied to cancel the development of this application for the time being, whilst it was fun to make, I currently don't see a reason to continue developing this further.

We were having trouble with various internal systems in the app as well as having trouble with optimization of the app between devices. These issues could've been fixed if enough time was spent on it, but I don't see the value in doing so.

My attention is focused on PC games and console platforms rather than mobile app development. The development of this game was a learning experience for all involved about the creation of mobile applications, but it's just not for us.
